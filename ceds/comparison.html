DVRPC

The Philadelphia region has five sectors with an LQ of 1.25 or higher: Educational Services (2.26), Management of Companies and Enterprises (1.32), Arts, Entertainment, and Recreation (1.32), Health Care and Social Assistance (1.29), and Finance and Insurance (1.26). These sectors account for 969,619 jobs, comprising 39 percent of the region’s total employment.

Two of the five competitive sectors have medium automation risks: Arts, Entertainment, and Recreation (54.9 percent) and Finance and Insurance (51.8 percent). These two sectors comprise 21.9 percent of the region’s competitive sector total employment. Three of the five competitive sectors have low automation risks: Educational Services (21.9 percent), Management of Companies and Enterprises (36.1 percent), and Health Care and Social Assistance (25.5 percent). These three sectors comprise 78.1 percent of the region’s competitive sector total employment.

Three of the five competitive sectors have high telework capacities: Educational Services (35 percent), Management of Companies and Enterprises (35.2 percent), Finance and Insurance (35.3 percent). These three sectors comprise 41.9 percent of the region’s competitive sector total employment. Two of the five competitive sectors have medium telework capacities: Arts, Entertainment, and Recreation (22 percent) and Health Care and Social Assistance (25.9 percent). These two sectors comprise 58.1 percent of the region’s competitive sector total employment.
